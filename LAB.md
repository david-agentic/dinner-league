## The discovery rules
- **Reality Rule.** Users are right about what they *experience*, often wrong about how to
  *solve* it. Observe the problem; design the solution; don't blindly implement requests.
- **Laughter Rule.** If people laugh together because of the app, protect that feature.
- **Phone Rule.** If someone passes their phone to another person because of the app, you
  built something social. Protect it.
- **Table Rule.** The app must never become the center of dinner. It improves the meal,
  then disappears. The table conversation always beats the screen.

## ⚠️ Flagged tension (Table Rule vs Khata Heist)
A game played at the table can *become* the activity instead of enhancing it. If Heist is
ever promoted from the Lab, it must be a waiting-room game that ends the instant food
arrives (the designed "Waiter Event"). If it can't disappear when the food does, it fails
the Table Rule and does not ship.
